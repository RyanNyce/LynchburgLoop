About the Loop

Hey, I'm Ryan — a 21-year-old Junior at Liberty University. I've been in the Lynchburg area since August 2023 for school and officially made it home in October 2025.

Lynchburg Loop is my way of sharing the hidden gems and underrated spots around the city that most people walk right past. Honest takes, real places, no fluff — starting with food and growing from there.

I also post video reviews on Instagram — follow along at @lynchburgloop.
